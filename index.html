How to Play Numberle

Objective

Guess the hidden number in limited attempts. Numbers can be 4-6 digits long depending on difficulty.

How to Play

  1. Choose difficulty (4-6 digits) using the buttons
  2. Enter your guess in the input field
  3. Submit your guess to get feedback
  4. Use color hints to improve your next guess
  5. Win by guessing correctly before attempts run out

Color Meanings

5

Green: Correct digit in correct position (2 dots = appears twice in solution)

3

Yellow: Digit exists in number but wrong position (1 dot = appears once)

8

Gray: Digit not in the number
